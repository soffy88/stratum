## 原文

The autumn rain fell softly on the old stone streets of Kyoto. Sayuri watched
from behind the latticed screen as a vermilion leaf drifted past the lantern.
She had waited three years for this moment, yet now that it had arrived, she
felt only a quiet, inexplicable sadness settle around her like the evening mist.
